Expungement & Record Sealing

  • Dismissed cases may qualify for expungement.
  • Deferred adjudication may qualify for nondisclosure.
  • Waiting periods apply depending on charge level.
  • No new convictions are required in most cases.

Important Legal Documents

  • State ID or Driver’s License
  • Social Security Card
  • Birth Certificate
  • Probation or parole paperwork
  • Court documents
