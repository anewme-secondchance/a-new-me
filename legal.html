You MAY qualify for Expungement if:

  • Case was dismissed
  • Found Not Guilty
  • No-billed by grand jury
  • Arrested but never charged (after waiting period)

You MAY qualify for Nondisclosure if:

  • Completed deferred adjudication
  • Completed probation for certain offenses
  • No new convictions during waiting period

‚ö† You likely CANNOT qualify if:

  • Family violence conviction
  • Sex offense conviction
  • Aggravated violent felony
  • Repeated convictions

Eligibility depends on charge type and case outcome.
