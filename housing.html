Housing Assistance

  • Transitional housing programs
  • Emergency shelters
  • Halfway houses
  • Permanent supportive housing
  • Reentry housing referrals

Important Documents Needed

  • State ID or Driver’s License
  • Social Security Card
  • Birth Certificate
  • Proof of Income
  • Proof of Address

Tips for Renting with a Record

  • Be honest but brief about your past.
  • Provide references if possible.
  • Show proof of stable income.
  • Offer a larger deposit if needed.
  • Stay professional and confident.
